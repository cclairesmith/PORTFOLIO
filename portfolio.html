PORTRAIT PHOTOGRAPHY

Photographer: Rachel Smith
Model: Will Smith

A simple black and white portrait of my younger brother came out to become my favorite portrait photo I have taken yet. I used the rule of thirds to position my subject on the left side of the frame whilst capturing a nice shadow drifting behind the subject. It's nice to work with close friends and family members when it comes to photography; of course you cannot make that work at all times, and in that case you must do your best to make sure both you and the client are comfortable.

Savannah Miller photographed by Rachel Smith

I have taken a liking to the dramatic effect that placing a black and white filter over a portrait
